Why They Marched: Untold Stories of the Women Who Fought for the Right to Vote

Susan Ware

Ware uncovers and explores the lesser known stories of women who contributed to the women's suffrage movement. Find yourself in awe of some of the simple and extraordinary measures behind the nineteen activists identified by Ware. Covering a range of stories from cookbooks to cartoonists, be prepared to find some conversation starters hidden within this gem.
