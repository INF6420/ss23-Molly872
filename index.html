About Molly

I'm so excited that you made it to this site! Welcome to Molly's Virtual Book Club, a place to connect with other bibliophiles in a virtual setting. I'm an avid reader, self-proclaimed bibliophile, and always looking to connect with other book lovers, which is why I made this site. Books update once per season, so you'll have more than enough time to accomplish your reading goals (no weekly requirements!).

You can revisit archived books and share comments so long as someone else is willing to join in. Joining a book club can be time-consuming and sometimes a little awkward. Here, you won't feel pressured to attend meetings and can freely share your thoughts. Share your favorite books with me using the Make a Recommendation Page, and join me on an adventure you'll never forget!
